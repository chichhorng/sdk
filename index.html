Our Aluminum Ingot Production

Environmentally friendly, Pure Aluminum Ingot production maderecycled materials

Our company, which started production on a 600 square meter area in İzmir Işıkkent Dökümcüler site in 2013, moved its new 6000 m2 facility ,3000 m2 of which is closed, 3000 m2 of open area,  in İzmir Torbalı Subaşı Heavy Industry Area.

In our facility, alloyed aluminum ingots are produced with rotary and reverberatory furnaces with a casting capacity of 10 tons in a single charge for businesses that make aluminum castings. All products processed in our business are guaranteed with all hazardous and non-hazardous recycling documents issued by the Ministry of Environment and Urbanization. Our company supplies raw materialsdomestic and abroad and has foreign import certificate.
