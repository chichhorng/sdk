Who We Are

WHO WE ARE

We are SDK Supply Co. Ltd, a leading advertising agency with over 16 years of experience in delivering creative, impactful, and high-quality advertising solutions. Based in Sri Lanka with plans to expand to Cambodia, we specialize in both indoor and outdoor advertising, providing innovative and results-driven strategies to help brands connect with their target audiences.

Founded by a team of experienced Sri Lankan professionals, SDK Supply Co. Ltd has a solid history of delivering high-quality construction and road infrastructure projects in Sri Lanka. Our expertise and dedication to excellence have enabled us to expand our operations into Cambodia, where we are now bringing our skills and commitment to new and exciting markets.

With deep industry knowledge and a focus on quality and efficiency, we handle projects of all scales from foundational construction to large-scale infrastructure. Each project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards, thanks to our talented and skilled team.

We are more than just builders; we are partners in progress, committed to creating infrastructure that supports growing communities and advances economies. At ABC Company, we take pride in building relationships, trust, and a lasting legacy across borders.
